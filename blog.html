Common AV Troubleshooting Mistakes and How to Fix Them

1. Using the Wrong Cables

Cheap or outdated HDMI and speaker cables cause poor audio/no signal, blurry video, or loss of ARC/eARC. We install high-speed, shielded cables rated for 4K, Dolby Atmos, and long runs.

2. Skipping Calibration

Out-of-box gear isn’t optimized. We calibrate TV picture (HDR/brightness/contrast), speaker levels and delay, and subwoofer placement/crossover.

3. Overloading the Network

Buffering or device drop-offs usually mean your network is maxed out. We assess capacity and upgrade routers, mesh, or hardwired lines.

4. Messy, Inaccessible Wiring

Unlabeled tangles make upgrades and fixes painful. We label, secure, and conceal wiring for clean serviceability.

When to Call SCV Tech

If your setup feels complicated, audio cuts out, devices disconnect, or you want to clean up years of DIY—call us. We’ll fix issues and future-proof the rest.

Pre-Wire vs. Retrofit: Which One Do You Need for Your AV Project?

Don’t start your AV project without reading this first. New build or existing home? That’s the key question when choosing between pre-wire and retrofit.

Pre-Wire: The Proactive Option

  • Happens before drywall is sealed
  • Full customization of speaker/AV/network lines
  • Clean installation and higher resale value

Retrofit: For Existing Homes

  • Wire without demolition
  • Discreet management with small access points
  • Great for camera upgrades or smart AV additions

Quick Guide

New Build✅ Pre-Wire
Remodel✅ Pre-Wire
Existing Home✅ Retrofit
