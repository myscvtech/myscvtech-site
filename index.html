FAQs

Can you install ceiling or in-wall speakers?
Yes! We install flush-mount speakers for a clean, professional look in homes and commercial spaces.

Do you offer custom home theater installations?
Absolutely. From projectors and screens to Dolby Atmos surround sound, we design immersive home theater setups.

Can I control my AV system from my phone or tablet?
Yes. We integrate AV systems with smartphone apps, smart remotes, and voice assistants like Alexa or Google Home.

Do you work with Sonos systems?
Yes. We install, configure, and integrate Sonos systems into your space, including Arc, Beam, and outdoor speakers.

Can you install AV systems in conference rooms or offices?
Yes. We provide displays, microphones, speakers, video conferencing tools, and Zoom-ready setups for businesses.

Can you hide all my AV equipment and wires?
Definitely. We offer in-wall cable routing and media closets so your setup stays clean and modern.

What’s the difference between NVR and DVR systems?
DVRs are used with analog cameras; NVRs are for IP cameras and offer better quality and smarter features.

Can I access my security footage from my smartphone?
Yes. Our systems include apps that allow you to view live or recorded footage from anywhere.

Do you provide video doorbells?
Yes. We install Ring, Nest, Wyze, and other video doorbells with mobile access and two-way audio.

Are your systems good for Airbnb or rental properties?
Yes. We install remote-access cameras and smart locks perfect for property management.

Do you provide monitoring services?
We offer self-monitoring and can integrate with third-party professional monitoring from the provider of your choice.

What is low voltage lighting?
It’s lighting that uses 12V or 24V systems, often used outdoors or for accent lighting. It’s safe, energy-efficient, and ideal for residential or commercial properties.

Can I control low voltage lighting with my phone?
Yes. We offer smart controllers that connect to your smartphone, Google Home, or Alexa.

Can you install pathway or landscape lights?
Yes. We install weatherproof LED systems perfect for walkways, backyards, and entry points.

Is low voltage lighting a good choice for my storefront or office?
Absolutely. It enhances curb appeal, improves safety, and saves on long-term energy costs.

How much does a basic security camera system cost?
A 4-camera HD system with installation typically starts around $1,200–$1,800, depending on features and location. Prices vary by job.

What’s the cost to mount a TV and hide cables?
Standard TV mounting with concealed wiring usually starts at $250–$450, depending on wall type and cable routing. Prices vary by job.

Do you charge hourly or by project?
Most jobs are quoted per project. Troubleshooting or small add-ons may be billed hourly.

Do you offer free quotes?
Yes. We provide free consultations and quotes for all installation services.

Do you offer bundle pricing?
Yes. We provide bundle discounts for combined services like AV, lighting, and security system installs.

Should I pre-wire during construction?
Yes—pre-wiring during new construction or remodeling is more cost-effective and future-proofs your space.

How many cameras do I need for full coverage?
Most homes need 4–6 cameras. Businesses may require 8–16+, depending on layout and entrances.

What’s the best AV system for a backyard?
We recommend weather-rated outdoor TVs and speakers, paired with surge protection and wireless app control.

Do you offer after-hours installation?
Yes. We can schedule installs during evenings or weekends to accommodate your schedule.
